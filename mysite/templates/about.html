About Me

As an information-hungry creature, I marvel at the fact that there has been no other time in history where the communication technology is so advanced that the access to encyclopedias, books, and scientific journals are near endless. With access to all of this information, I plan to gain a mastery of mind over the material world and to generate new ideas. This is the daunting task of the inventor who is often misunderstood. Without inventors, the human race would have long ago perished in the endless war against pitiless elemental forces.

The purpose of this site is for me to understand what I really believe and then to invite critics in order to better my thoughts. I've stalled the development of this site on multiple occasions out of fear of being wrong, but now I understand that in order for me to get to the truth I must brutally challenge all assumptions and lay them at the doorsteps of my readers.
